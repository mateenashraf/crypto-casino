Player FAQ

Should I use a VPN to play from another country?

We do not provide VPN links or encourage bypassing regional restrictions. You are responsible for following local laws where you play. If you travel abroad, connect from a permitted jurisdiction. Using a VPN to hide your location may violate our terms and local regulations.

Do you accept Hong Kong or China digital currency (e-CNY / digital HKD)?

Central bank digital currencies (e-CNY, HKMA digital HKD) are not directly accepted today. You can play with ETH, SOL, USDT, USDC, and TRX on supported networks. We monitor regulatory updates for future CBDC integration.

Can I play slots and roulette too?

Yes. Scroll to the Casino Floor for NeonDraw Slots and American Roulette. Both use your casino wallet balance — deposit once, then spin the reels or the wheel.

Is this like the lottery I already play?

Yes, same format: pick 6 numbers from 1 to 49, wait for the scheduled draw, and check if you won. The difference is you buy with crypto and get paid to your wallet instead of claiming at a retailer.

How do I buy a ticket?

Connect MetaMask or any Web3 wallet, pick your numbers, choose a ticket ($10–$1,000 or custom), and confirm. Your full ticket price goes to the prize pool.

Who pays blockchain network fees (gas)?

You do — in your own wallet when MetaMask asks you to confirm. That fee goes to the Ethereum network (validators/miners), not to NeonDraw. We do not charge it, keep it, or subsidize it. Your ticket or deposit amount goes 100% to the prize pool or casino balance. Fees change with network traffic and are often much lower than peak estimates. Slots and roulette use your deposited balance with no extra gas per spin.

How fast do winners get paid?

Crypto payouts are sent directly to your wallet after the draw. No waiting weeks for a check, most players see funds within minutes.

Can I prove I bought a ticket?

Absolutely. Use Ticket Lookup to see every entry linked to your wallet, with links to the blockchain transaction.

What are free bonus tickets?

They’re exactly what they sound like, a free re-entry if you win one in a draw. Redeem them with no payment, same numbers or a new line.

Is NeonDraw licensed?

Yes. We operate under a Curaçao Gaming Control Board interactive gaming & lottery license. See the full certificate in our License section.

Play Responsibly

Lottery and casino play should be fun, never a way to solve money problems. NeonDraw is for adults 18+ only.

  • Set a budget before you play and stick to it, like you would at a casino floor.
  • Never chase losses. Tomorrow’s draw is another chance.
  • Take breaks. If playing stops feeling fun, step away.
